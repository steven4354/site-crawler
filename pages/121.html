IDC MarketScape Names Tipalti a Worldwide Leader

In SaaS and Cloud-Enabled Midmarket Accounts Payable Applications

According to IDC, the COVID-19 pandemic has simultaneously halted and changed society. We are living in unprecedented times of uncertainty and volatility. The first priority for companies is to shore up their money and financial processes quickly. For many companies, the core issue with spending is complexity, which comes in multiple layerswith many companies dealing with multiple business units, spend types, and countries. If you dont have the tools, you cant have a holistic view of accounts payable (AP)which hinders both growth and success.

The IDC MarketScape evaluated eleven SaaS and cloud-enabled AP vendors in the market using a wide range of criteria for evaluation, including product functionality, range of services, customer satisfaction, service delivery, cloud capabilities, technical architecture, innovation, and growth.

Tipalti is proud to be a Worldwide Leader in the IDC MarketScape report. Download the report excerpt to learn more about IDC MarketScapes guidance for mid-market businesses looking for an AP solution.
