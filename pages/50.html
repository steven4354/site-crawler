Why trust Tipalti with your ad network?

Eliminate Manual Processes

Manual effort can lead to publisher payment errors. Wipe out 80% of the payment workload with publisher self-service, automated communications, and unified banking interface.

Build a Global Publisher Network

Tipalti pays to 196 countries and over 120 local currencies from a single point over multiple payment methods (PayPal, US ACH, wire transfer, check, Global ACH, prepaid debit).

Effortless Tax Compliance

Dont wait until year-end to hunt down publishers for their tax forms. Collect W-9, W-8 series, or VAT ID forms and produce submission-ready 1099 and 1042-S reports.

Improve Ad Network Quality

How Adtech Companies Use Tipalti

  • Publishers provide identity, payment details, and tax information (e.g. W8, W9, VAT).
  • Tipalti verifies payment details using over 26,000 rules.
  • The portal provides updates on payment status and allows for self-service changes.
Publishers login into a self-service secure web portal
  • Based on your performance data, you can create a payfile or trigger payments from our API.
  • Tipalti AI routes payment requests to your approvers.
Your tracking systems determine payments
  • Once approved, Tipalti makes sure funds exist and pays in the publishers chosen payment method (e.g. ACH, wire transfer, PayPal, etc.).
  • No more logging into individual banking portals or reconciling payments from across multiple accounts.
  • Tipalti sends the payments out through the proper banking channels.
  • Tipalti alerts payees of payment status and reports any issues to them if funds dont land.
  • Payments and transaction fees are fully reported for reconciliation in real time.
